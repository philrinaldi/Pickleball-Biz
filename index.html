Our Mission

Brio Pickleball Club is more than a place to play—it's a community. We bring people of all ages together to stay active, connect and thrive. Located at The Boulevard at The Brio in Yaphank, we offer a high‑quality, climate‑controlled facility with six professional courts, comfortable lounge spaces and programs for every skill level.

Community‑Focused

Our club fosters friendships and social connections through leagues, mixers and events.

Pro‑Quality Courts

Six regulation indoor courts with cushioned surfaces, LED lighting and ample space.

Programs for All

Beginner clinics, youth camps, private lessons, leagues and tournaments ensure everyone has a place on the court.
